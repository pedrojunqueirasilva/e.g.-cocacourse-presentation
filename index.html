CocaCourse

Innovation in Learning & Talent Management at Coca-Cola

Overview | Motivation | Problems Solved | How it Works | Why it Works | Future & Expansion | Final Considerations

Overview

The CocaCourse platform is an AI-powered learning and development ecosystem based on Artificial Intelligence (AI), created specifically for Coca-Cola employees. Its objective is to transform the experience of training, professional growth, and performance evaluation into a personalized, intelligent, and constantly evolving journey.

Motivation

The development of CocaCourse arose from the need to:

  • Respond to the increasing demand for accelerated upskilling and reskilling.
  • Modernize Training & Development (T&D) and Performance Management processes.
  • Make learning more attractive, personalized, and effective.
  • Reduce training costs and time with the use of AI.
  • Integrate learning into the workflow, increasing productivity.
  • Promote a culture of continuous, collaborative, and data-driven learning.

Problems Solved

CocaCourse addresses the following challenges:

  • Generic and outdated training solutions.
  • Lack of visibility into individual skills and skill gaps.
  • Low efficiency and engagement in development programs.
  • Subjective and infrequent performance evaluations.
  • Misalignment between individual and strategic company objectives.
  • Difficulty in identifying talents and potential successors.

How it Works

CocaCourse operates on four main pillars:

  1. Employee Data Collection: Gathering current skills, performance reviews, career goals, and learning preferences through integration with HR systems (e.g., Workday, SuccessFactors) and dynamic forms.
  2. Intelligent Analysis with AI: Analyzing skill gaps, mapping personalized career paths, and using AI algorithms (machine learning, NLP, collaborative filtering) to understand employee profiles and contexts.
  3. Personalized Recommendations: Suggesting courses, articles, videos, and practical projects; indicating internal mentors and knowledge application opportunities; aligning with career paths and organizational needs.
  4. Continuous Monitoring and Improvement: Evaluating success and learning indicators; dynamically adjusting content and recommendations based on results; continuously learning from new data with the AI engine.

Why it Works

  • Utilizes AI for personalization at scale, promoting greater engagement and learning retention.
  • Promotes continuous feedback and adjustments, improving the learning experience.
  • Reduces training time and cost with high effectiveness.
  • Strengthens the culture of growth and career ownership.
  • Integrates with Coca-Cola's strategy of innovation, sustainability, and talent development.

Future & Expansion

  • Incorporation of predictive AI for talent identification, turnover risk, and succession planning.
  • Integration with immersive technologies (VR/AR) for more realistic and applied training.
  • Development of coaching and real-time feedback bots.

Final Considerations

CocaCourse is more than a platform: it is a proposal for cultural and strategic transformation of talent management at Coca-Cola, based on data, technology, and focus on the human element. Its implementation strengthens the company's positioning as a brand that values growth, equity, and innovation with human impact.
